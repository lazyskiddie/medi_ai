Capabilities

Built for
Accuracy.

01 — Connection
Triple Protocol Support

Bluetooth BLE, USB Serial, and WiFi HTTP all supported simultaneously. The app connects to whichever protocol your device speaks.

02 — Intelligence
Claude AI Diagnostic Engine

Powered by Anthropic's Claude — analyzes all vitals together with patient symptoms for differential diagnosis, not just individual readings.

03 — Alerts
Abnormal Value Detection

Every reading is automatically flagged against clinical normal ranges. Color-coded indicators warn of borderline and critical values in real time.

04 — Report
Full Clinical Report

Primary diagnosis, severity rating, clinical reasoning, differential diagnoses, recommended tests, immediate actions, and emergency red flags.

05 — Demo
Simulation Mode

No hardware yet? Every instrument has a Demo mode that simulates realistic live readings — test the full system before your devices arrive.

06 — Privacy
Runs in Your Browser

No data stored on any server. All hardware communication is direct from your browser to your device. Patient data never leaves your machine.
